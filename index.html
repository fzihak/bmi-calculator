Understanding Your BMI

Your BMI helps assess your health risks based on your height and weight. A balanced diet and regular exercise can improve BMI. Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

Healthy BMI Tips: Aim to keep your BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 for optimal health. Regular physical activity and a balanced diet can help maintain this range.
